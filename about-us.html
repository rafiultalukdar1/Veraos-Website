Revolutionizing Global Intelligence

Veraos is transforming the way the world accesses intelligence, unifying trillions of data points into a single, powerful platform. Our mission is to empower law enforcement, government agencies, and security professionals with real-time, AI-driven intelligence that enhances investigations, safeguards national security, and prevents threats before they happen.

The Problem We Solve

For decades, intelligence has been fragmented and inefficient, with critical information buried in disconnected databases. Delayed access to data has led to missed threats, failed investigations, and preventable crises. Veraos eliminates these barriers by delivering real-time, crossreferenced intelligence insights at an unprecedented scale.

Law Enforcement – Criminal investigations, suspect tracking, and case resolution.

Government & National Security – Counterterrorism, threat intelligence, and border control.

Financial & Corporate Security – Fraud detection, risk assessment, and regulatory compliance.

Legal & Investigative Services – Background checks, asset searches, and litigation support.

Small Business Security – Real-time alerts, identity verification, and storefront protection through smart data integrations.

Our Technology

Veraos is built on cutting-edge AI and data intelligence, with two flagship technologies
powering our platform:

Neural Ocean – The World's Largest Investigative Data Lake

Neural Ocean is a global intelligence data lake, continuously expanding across 150+ countries with trillions of data points. It provides:

  • Real-Time Data Access – Continuously updated with new intelligence sources
  • Multi-Dimensional Search – Cross-references criminal records, financial transactions, identities, and more.
  • Actionable Insights – Raw data transformed into meaningful intelligence for investigations.

Overwatch – AI-Powered Global Intelligence Search

Overwatch is Veraos' Large Intelligence Model (LIM), designed to conduct natural languagedriven investigationsacross Neural Ocean.

  • Advanced AI Interpretation – Understands and refines search queries like an investigator.
  • Deep Context Awareness – Recognizes aliases, connections, and evolving threat patterns.
  • Predictive Intelligence – Anticipates risks based on historical data trends.
  • Automated Report Generation – Delivers structured, intelligence-packed reports instantly.

Our Story

Veraos is the result of decades of experience, innovation, and the pursuit of a
unified intelligence solution.

The Foundation of Innovation

Veraos founder Joseph J. Penora has a history of building technology that solves critical problems. As the creator of Friend Verifier, a groundbreaking tool that analyzed social media data for background screening, and Gatsby, a social platform designed to elevate user security and privacy, preventing individuals from being targeted by criminals, Joseph has consistently leveraged data intelligence to drive success.

In parallel, Intelldata, founded by Joseph's father, Joseph S. Penora, has been a trusted investigative data provider for over 40 years, working as a subcontractor for federal agencies since 9/11. Intelldata specialized in providing raw data intelligence to support national security operations, aiding federal investigations with vast datasets.

The Turning Point: Identifying a National Security Gap

By August 2024, after extensive discussions with intelligence operatives, it became evident that federal agencies were struggling to acquire and utilize raw data effectively. They were handcuffed by stringent regulations such as FISMA (Federal Information Security Management Act) and FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation), making it difficult to leverage real-time intelligence for national security. These bureaucratic hurdles delayed access to critical data, putting national security at risk.

Recognizing the urgent need for a faster, AI-driven, and regulatory-compliant intelligence solution, Veraos was born—merging decades of Intelldata's investigative expertise with cutting-edge AI to create Neural Ocean and Overwatch.

Today, Veraos stands as a next-generation intelligence company, providing real-time, AIpowered search and analysis, ensuring that law enforcement and government agencies are never left in the dark. We bring together decades of experience with cutting-edge AI innovation to create the most powerful intelligence solution available today.
